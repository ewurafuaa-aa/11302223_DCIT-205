Introduction to Web Development

Instructor: Michael Soli

Duration: 8 Weeks

Next Session: January 15, 2023

Price: GHS3,000

Location: University Of Ghana, Department of Computer Science, Graduate School Room 2

Course Description

Dive into the dynamic realm of web development with our immersive course. From HTML to JavaScript, learn the building blocks of modern web design and functionality. Discover the art of crafting responsive and visually appealing websites while mastering the tools that power the internet. Whether you are a novice or aiming to level up your skills, join us on this coding adventure to create stunning, interactive web experiences. Elevate your digital presence and unleash your creativity in the world of web development.
