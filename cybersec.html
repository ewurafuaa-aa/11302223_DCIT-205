Cyber Security & Ethical Hacking

Instructor: Mark Atta Mensah

Duration: 6 Weeks

Next Session: January 15, 2023

Price: GHS3,000

Location: University Of Ghana, Department of Computer Science, Graduate School Room 1

Course Description

Embark on a journey into the world of cybersecurity with our comprehensive course on ethical hacking. Unveil the mysteries of digital defense as you learn to safeguard systems, detect vulnerabilities, and fortify networks against cyber threats. Delve into the ethical hacker's mindset, mastering the tools and techniques used to assess an enhance security. Whether you are a beginner or an IT professional, join us to acquire the skills needed to protect and defend in the ever-evolving landscape of digital security.
