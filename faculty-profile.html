Dr. Prof. Ebenezar Owusu

DEAN, DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

Email: ebenowusu98@ug.edu.gh

Phone: +233 20 645 7678

Research Interests: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning

Biography

Dr. Ebenezer Owusu is a distinguished lecturer in the Computer Science Department, bringing a wealth of expertise and passion to the realm of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML). Born and raised in Ghana, Dr. Owusu's journey in the world of technology began at an early age, fueled by an insatiable curiosity about the potential of computers.

Ebenezer's academic journey started with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Ghana, where he quickly distinguished himself as a bright and dedicated student. His interest in AI and ML blossomed during his undergraduate years, and he undertook several research projects exploring the intersection of these fields.

After completing his bachelor's degree, Dr. Owusu pursued a master's degree in Computer Science with a focus on machine learning algorithms for natural language processing. His research during this period earned him accolades and set the stage for his continued exploration of cutting-edge technologies.

With a desire to deepen his knowledge and contribute to the global discourse on AI and ML, Dr. Owusu pursued a Ph.D. in Computer Science at a leading research university. His doctoral thesis, titled "Advancements in Deep Learning for Image Recognition," showcased his innovative approach to solving complex problems in the realm of computer vision.

Throughout his academic journey, Dr. Owusu has not only been committed to advancing the frontiers of knowledge but has also been dedicated to mentorship. Many of his students fondly recall his engaging teaching style and his ability to make complex topics accessible. He is known for fostering a collaborative learning environment that encourages students to explore and question.

As a lecturer in the Computer Science Department, Dr. Owusu continues to inspire the next generation of computer scientists. His courses on AI and ML are known for their blend of theoretical foundations and practical applications. Students appreciate his hands-on approach, often working on real-world projects that mirror the challenges faced in the industry.

Beyond the classroom, Dr. Owusu is an active researcher, publishing in reputable journals and presenting at international conferences. His work often delves into novel applications of AI in healthcare, natural language processing, and computer vision. He collaborates with both industry partners and fellow academics to push the boundaries of what AI and ML can achieve.

Dr. Ebenezer Owusu's impact extends beyond academia. He is involved in community outreach programs, organizing workshops to introduce AI concepts to local schools and communities. His commitment to inclusivity and diversity is reflected in his efforts to encourage underrepresented groups to pursue careers in technology.

In the ever-evolving landscape of AI and ML, Dr. Owusu remains at the forefront, embracing the challenges and opportunities that arise. His dedication to research, teaching, and community engagement embodies the spirit of a visionary educator shaping the future of computer science and artificial intelligence.
